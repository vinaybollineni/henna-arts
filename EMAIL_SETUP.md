## Troubleshooting

### Not Receiving Emails?

1. **Check Spam/Junk Folder**
   - Formspree emails might go to spam initially
   - Mark them as "Not Spam" to receive future emails in inbox

2. **Verify Email Confirmation**
   - Make sure you clicked the confirmation link in the first email from Formspree

3. **Test the Form**
   - Fill it out yourself to ensure it's working
   - Use a different email address as the sender

4. **Check Email Address**
   - Verify meghnahennaart@gmail.com is correct and accessible

### Form Not Submitting?

1. Make sure all fields are filled out
2. Check that the email format is valid
3. Try a different browser
4. Check browser console for errors (F12 ‚Üí Console tab)
